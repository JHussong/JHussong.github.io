{ About Me: }




My name is Joe and I am currently on the difficult journey of learning how to code. In the last decade I have twice found myself unemployed and trying to figure out what to do next. The first time was a company closure and the second time was a company deciding to do things differently. With the last instance of job loss I decided that from now on things will be different and I will make the rules. I decided to take the plunge into the world of programming. It is a way for for me to gain skills that will allow me to never worry again about losing a job and also a new way to create something. On the rare chance that I actually leave my computer and this process of learning, I can most likely be found taking pictures with either a Sony camera or my phone. I find the creation of any kind of art to be a small way to make the universe a better place.
